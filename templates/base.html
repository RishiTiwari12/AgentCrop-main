Privacy

Agent Crop respects your privacy. Your uploaded Images get deleted after an hour. It doesn't have any annoying ads so that you can predict Crops Diseases without any interruption.

Privacy-First Features:

  • No Data Collection
  • No Cookies stored in your browser
  • Automatic deletion of your Images
  • No Ads
